What is Microbit?

"Microbit is a tiny programmable computer, designed to make learning and teaching easy and fun!"



Our Project

For this microbit project, our goal was to make a controller. Our group was successful in making a functional controller using code blocks from the microbit website.

We explored the microbit and its functions before we started the project. We first learned to control the servo with the microbit, connecting the wires between the servo and the microbit. Next we tried controlling the servo wirelessly by sending a signal between microbit 1 and microbit 2, connecting the wires into microbit 2 and controlling it with microbit 1.
